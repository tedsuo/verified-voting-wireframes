Verified Voting

VerifiedVoting.org champions reliable and publicly verifiable elections in the United States. The right to have one's vote counted properly is a cornerstone of our democratic system. Making sure that our election systems are reliable and publicly verifiable enfranchises voters and increases public confidence and participation in our political process.
